Detailed Description

This class represents a piece of baggage associated with a flight booking.

The Baggage class encapsulates information about a piece of baggage, including its unique ID, the username of the owner, airport of origin, airport of destination, check-in date and time, and weight in pounds. Objects of this class can be used to store and retrieve baggage-related information in the WrightFlightManager application.
